When was the last time you discovered and fell in love with a Podcast?

At Enki, we have a single mission: To play and discover podcasts you'll love.

Since we started back in January, we have been hard at work on the aggregating data for our Neo4j database and our collaborative filtering algorithms. It's the most comprehensive analysis of podcasts ever undertaken (by us).
